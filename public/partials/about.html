About Marci



Marci grew up in Wethersfield, Connecticut. She was a local news anchor and reporter for 10 years before ditching the newsroom to teach yoga, write books, and blog for the Houston Chronicle and other Hearst Publications. She loves cats, fitness, and spirituality.

Marci has an awesome website where she posts short videos with tips to help us feel our best. She wants to have five kids who are equally good at math and feelings.

About Sajid



Sajid was born in Fort Worth, Texas, but he grew up all over the world with stints in Egypt, Malaysia, India, and Bangladesh.

After graduating from Duke, he went to Harvard Law School and got his first job as an associate at a big Houston firm. And he was miserable. So after a year, he left the law and took a job as a consultant for the Boston Consulting Group in Dallas. This was a great fit for Sajid. He loves data, PowerPoint, and travel. But after a few years of no sleep and being away from home all the time, he went to work for the family business.

These days, when Sajid isn’t at the office with his family, on a business trip to London or Romania, or at the gym (just kidding), he is busy fantasizing about his next trip, being a crazy cat dad, or trying to talk Marci into having five kids. She’s not budging.
